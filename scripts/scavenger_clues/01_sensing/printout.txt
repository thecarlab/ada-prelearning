SENSING MODULE

Job: collect raw measurements from the world and the ego vehicle.

Common sensors:
- Cameras measure light, color, lanes, signs, signals, and object appearance.
- LiDAR measures 3D distance points around the vehicle.
- Radar measures distance and relative speed, often working well in rain or fog.
- GPS, IMU, and wheel odometry measure vehicle position and motion clues.

Key idea: sensing does not understand the scene yet. It creates time-stamped data for the rest of the AV stack.
