LOCALIZATION MODULE

Job: estimate where the ego vehicle is.

Localization tracks:
- position on the road or map
- heading direction
- speed and motion
- lane-level location when high-definition maps are available

Common inputs include GPS, IMU, wheel odometry, camera or LiDAR map matching, and sometimes SLAM.

Key idea: localization answers "Where am I?" Planning and control need this pose before they can choose and follow a safe path.
