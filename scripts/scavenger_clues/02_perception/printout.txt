PERCEPTION MODULE

Job: turn raw sensor data into useful facts about the driving scene.

Perception may detect:
- lanes and road edges
- traffic lights and traffic signs
- cars, trucks, bikes, and pedestrians
- drivable space, curbs, cones, and obstacles
- 3D object boxes, object tracks, and free space

Key idea: perception answers "What is around me?" It often fuses camera, LiDAR, and radar data so the vehicle has a stronger view than any one sensor alone.
